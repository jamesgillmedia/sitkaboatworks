Sitka Boatworks

Sitka Boatworks is a company of wooden boatbuilders and shipwrights located in Sitka, Alaska. We build, restore and repair wooden boats of all kinds. Our team of experienced craftsmen uses traditional techniques and the finest materials to create boats that are not only beautiful but also seaworthy and durable. Our Services

Boat Building

We build wooden boats of all kinds, from small dinghies to large sailboats and motor yachts. Our craftsmen use traditional techniques and the finest materials to create boats that are not only beautiful but also seaworthy and durable.

Restoration

We restore wooden boats to their former glory. Whether your boat needs new planks, ribs, or a complete refit, we have the expertise to make it seaworthy again.

Repair

We repair wooden boats of all kinds, from minor dings and scratches to major structural damage. Our team of experienced shipwrights can get your boat back on the water in no time.
